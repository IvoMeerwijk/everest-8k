The Mission

To prove that movement is the ultimate medicine. We are running 8.8km through the streets of Amsterdam— one-thousandth of the height of Mt. Everest (8,848m)—to fuel a journey from the canals of the Netherlands to the highest peak on Earth.

The Cause

This isn’t just a bakery run; it’s a stand against Ankylosing Spondylitis (AS). Our founder is currently finishing the Seven Summits to show that a diagnosis doesn’t define your altitude.

We run to celebrate mobility, to support those with chronic “fusing” conditions, and to enjoy the best artisanal fuel Amsterdam has to offer. 50% of all proceeds will go directly to ReumaNederland to fund research and support for those living with inflammatory arthritis.
